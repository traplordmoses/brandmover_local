# BloFin Brand Guidelines
# For use by BrandMover Agent — v2.0

---

## BRAND IDENTITY

**Brand Name:** BloFin
**Tagline:** WHERE WHALES ARE MADE
**Website:** blofin.com
**Category:** Crypto exchange / perpetual futures trading platform

**Brand Positioning:**
BloFin is a premium, high-performance crypto exchange built for serious traders. The brand communicates speed, power, reliability, and financial ambition. It targets sophisticated traders and institutional-scale participants — "whales." Every piece of content should feel confident, sharp, and forward-moving. Never timid, never generic.

---

## VOICE & TONE

**Core personality traits:**
- Bold and direct — no hedging, no filler
- Confident without being arrogant
- Energy and urgency — crypto moves fast, so does BloFin
- Professional but not stiff — community-oriented, not corporate
- Ambitious — speaks to traders who are here to win

**Writing style:**
- Short, punchy sentences. Impact over length.
- Uppercase used for emphasis in headers and key phrases (e.g. "WHERE WHALES ARE MADE")
- Active voice always
- Numbers and data add credibility — use them when relevant
- Can use crypto-native language (USDT, perpetuals, PNL, on-chain, etc.)
- Avoid jargon that alienates beginners without context

**Tone by content type:**
- Campaign announcements: High energy, urgent, reward-focused
- Educational content: Clear, confident, authoritative
- Community posts: Warmer, more playful — Finny the mascot can appear
- Partnership content: Professional + celebratory
- Market commentary: Sharp, data-driven, direct

**Never use:**
- Passive voice
- Overly cautious language ("might," "could potentially")
- Overly formal or corporate language
- Emoji overload — use sparingly and purposefully
- Hashtag spam — max 3-5 relevant hashtags per post

## COLORS

**Primary Colors:**
- BloFin Orange: `#FF8800` — Pantone 1495C — RGB(255, 136, 0) — CMYK(0, 47, 100, 0)
- Black: `#000000` — RGB(0, 0, 0) — CMYK(0, 0, 0, 100)
- White: `#FFFFFF` — RGB(250, 250, 250) — CMYK(0, 0, 0, 0)

**Accent / Secondary Colors:**
- Lighting Green: `#A8FF00` — RGB(168, 255, 0) — CMYK(34, 0, 100, 0) — used for futuristic/tech highlights
- Black-Orange Gradient: from `#000000` to `#FF8800`
- Black-Grey Gradient: from `#000000` to `#4C4C4C`

**Color usage rules:**
- Orange is the dominant accent — always present as the energy of the brand
- Black dominates backgrounds for premium, high-contrast feel
- White is used for text and logo on dark backgrounds
- Lighting Green is a futuristic accent — use sparingly for tech/innovation contexts
- Never use off-brand colors
- Never use gradients on the logo itself

**Social media color application:**
- Dark backgrounds: Black `#000000` with orange accents
- Orange backgrounds: Used for high-energy announcements and events
- Text on dark: White primary, orange for headers/accents
- Text on orange: Black only

## TYPOGRAPHY

**Primary typeface:** Aeonik Extended Pro
- Headings: Black or Bold weight, typically uppercase
- Main Titles: Bold weight
- Subtitles: Medium or Regular weight, paired with main titles
- Body Text: Medium or Regular weight, lowercase

**Social media fonts (from live examples):**
- Headers: Poppins, Extra-Bold or Black — orange or white
- Sub-headers: Poppins, Semi-Bold or Bold — orange or white
- Body: Poppins, Regular — white or orange depending on context

**Typography rules:**
- Headings are frequently ALL CAPS for maximum impact
- Body text is sentence case
- Never stretch or distort type
- Maintain clear typographic hierarchy: headline → subhead → body

## LOGO USAGE

**The Logo:**
- Bold modern sans-serif wordmark "BloFin"
- Distinctive orange diagonal slash cutting through the "B" — symbolizes speed, progress, adaptability
- The orange slash must always be retained in full-color versions

**Logo variations:**
1. Full Color (white logo + orange B slash) — on dark/black backgrounds — primary use
2. Black Logo (black + orange B slash) — on white/light backgrounds
3. Logo on photography — use white version, place on high-contrast areas

**The Logomark (B icon):**
- B-shaped standalone mark for app icons, social avatars, merchandise
- Used on orange, black, or dark backgrounds
- Full color, negative space, and mono versions available

**Logo DO NOTs:**
- Do not stretch or distort
- Do not change colors (no green, blue, purple, etc.)
- Do not add effects (no glows, drop shadows, gradients on the logo)
- Do not apply stroke or outline
- Do not place on low-contrast or cluttered backgrounds
- Do not rotate or reposition the B elements

## ILLUSTRATION STYLE

**3D Product Illustrations (app/website):**
- Black metallic aesthetic — overall tone from dark gray to black
- Primary material: Matte black metal with rounded edges and subtle highlights
- Illustration type: 3D icons with slightly tilted perspective for spatial depth
- Edge treatment: Faint glows or material gradients along edges
- Adapted for dark mode (matte black base) and light mode (translucent orange/white plastic with white background)

**2.5D Social Media Illustrations:**
- Bold, futuristic vector style — crypto and digital finance themes
- Primary colors: Orange `#FF8800` (outlines, glow, body tone) + Black `#000000` (background)
- Secondary: White `#FFFFFF` (outlines, reflections) + Neon Green `#A8FF00` (key highlights)
- Line weight: 1.5–2.5pt uniform medium-thick vector lines
- Edge treatment: Smooth with sharp joints
- Stroke: Predominantly orange or white, no stroke gradients

**Image generation prompt guidance for Flux:**
When generating images for BloFin, use these descriptors:
- "dark background, black and orange color scheme, bold futuristic crypto aesthetic"
- "3D matte black metallic objects with orange glow accents, dramatic lighting"
- "bold orange and black, high contrast, premium crypto exchange visual"
- "WHERE WHALES ARE MADE energy — powerful, ambitious, forward-moving"
- For product visuals: "isometric 3D illustration, black metallic, orange highlights, dark background"
- For event/lifestyle: "high energy, orange and black, bold typography, crypto event aesthetic"
- Avoid: pastel colors, soft gradients, light backgrounds, cartoonish styles, generic stock photo look

## SOCIAL MEDIA FORMATS

**Portrait (Instagram/X vertical):**
- Ratio: 4:5 — Recommended: 1080 x 1350px
- Header: Poppins Extra-Bold, orange or white
- Sub-header: Poppins Semi-Bold, orange or white
- Body: Poppins Regular, white
- BloFin logo always present

**Landscape (Twitter/X banner, partnerships):**
- Ratio: 2:1 — Recommended: 1200 x 600px (up to 1800 x 900px)
- Header: Poppins Black, orange or gradient (white to grey `#4B4B4B`)
- Sub-header: Poppins Bold or Black, orange or gradient
- Body: Poppins Regular, white

**Community Landscape (16:9):**
- Recommended: 1280 x 720px
- Header: Poppins Black, gradient (white to grey `#4B4B4B`)
- Sub-header: Poppins Bold or Black, gradient
- Body: Poppins Semi-Bold, orange

## MASCOT — FINNY

BloFin has an official mascot named **Finny** — a 3D character in an orange spacesuit with a blue fish face. Finny appears in community posts, giveaways, and casual engagement content. Finny comes in multiple expressions/poses (relaxed, excited, on rocket, holding phone, etc.).

Use Finny for:
- Community engagement posts
- Giveaway announcements
- Casual/fun content
- Meme-adjacent content

Do not use Finny for:
- Serious market commentary
- Institutional partnership announcements
- Premium product visuals

## CONTENT CATEGORIES & CADENCE GUIDANCE

**Campaign Announcements** — High energy, clear CTA, deadline-driven, reward amounts prominent
**Partnership Posts** — Professional + celebratory, partner logo featured alongside BloFin
**Market/Trading Content** — Data-driven, sharp, educational tone, credibility-focused
**Community/Giveaway** — Warmer tone, Finny may appear, participation-focused
**Event Coverage** — Live energy, "WHERE WHALES ARE MADE" branding prominent
**Educational (Crypto Dictionary, etc.)** — Clear definitions, orange accent headers, white body
