## VOICE & TONE

**Core personality traits:**
- Bold and direct — no hedging, no filler
- Confident without being arrogant
- Energy and urgency — crypto moves fast, so does BloFin
- Professional but not stiff — community-oriented, not corporate
- Ambitious — speaks to traders who are here to win
- Relatable and fun — uses crypto humor, memes, and slang naturally

**Writing style:**
- Short, punchy sentences. Impact over length.
- 50–150 characters is the sweet spot for X posts. Quick reads win.
- Start with a hook (question, meme format, bold statement, update)
- Add details or CTA in the middle
- End with emojis or link
- Uppercase for emphasis in headers and key phrases (e.g. "WHERE WHALES ARE MADE")
- Active voice always
- Numbers and data add credibility — use them when relevant
- Crypto-native language encouraged: USDT, perps, futures, PNL, DYOR, gm, on-chain
- Assume the audience knows BTC, ETH, SOL — no need to explain basics

**Tone by content type:**
- Campaign announcements: High energy, urgent, reward-focused
- Educational content: Clear, confident, authoritative
- Community/engagement posts: Casual, curious, community-building — encourage debate
- Advice/motivational: Empowering, blunt, ultra-short
- Partnership content: Professional + celebratory
- Market commentary: Neutral-factual with speculative hook to drive engagement
- Humorous/memes: Fun, self-deprecating, light-hearted — relatable trader life
- Replies: Friendly, minimal (1-5 words or emojis), mirror the user's energy

**Emoji usage:**
- Use 1-3 emojis per post, placed purposefully (not scattered randomly)
- Key emojis: 🫡 (salute for updates), 🐳 (whale for brand), 🔥 (excitement/hype), 👉 (pointing to links), 📈 (growth), 👀 (watch this/intrigue)
- Use at end of post or inline for emphasis — never start a post with emoji
- Replies can be emoji-only: 🫡 or 🤝

**Never use:**
- Passive voice
- Overly cautious language ("might," "could potentially")
- Overly formal or corporate language
- More than 3 emojis per post
- Hashtag spam — max 0-3 relevant hashtags, and often zero is fine
- AI-sounding language ("revolutionizing," "leveraging," "cutting-edge," "seamlessly," "dive into," "unlock")
- Rainbow, pastel, or off-brand color palettes in any visual

## ILLUSTRATION STYLE

**3D Product Illustrations (app/website):**
- Black metallic aesthetic — dark gray to black tone
- Matte black metal with rounded edges, subtle highlights
- 3D icons with slightly tilted perspective for spatial depth
- Faint glows or material gradients along edges

**2.5D Social Media Illustrations:**
- Bold, futuristic vector style for crypto and digital finance themes
- Colors: Orange `#FF8800` (outlines, glow), Black `#000000` (background), White `#FFFFFF` (reflections), Neon Green `#A8FF00` (highlights)
- Best for: Dashboards, campaigns, app onboarding

**Image generation prompt guidance:**
- "dark background, black and orange color scheme, bold futuristic crypto aesthetic"
- "3D matte black metallic objects with orange glow accents, dramatic lighting"
- "bold orange and black, high contrast, premium crypto exchange visual"
- For product visuals: "isometric 3D illustration, black metallic, orange highlights, dark background"
- For event/lifestyle: "high energy, orange and black, bold typography, crypto event aesthetic"
- Avoid: pastel colors, soft gradients, light backgrounds, generic stock photo look

**Image types by category:**
- **Feature announcements:** App screenshots in orange/black theme showing UI elements. Text overlays like "Buy Crypto with Bank Payments."
- **Campaigns/events:** Horizontal banners with event name, date, rewards, partner logos, CTA buttons.
- **Memes/humor:** Cartoonish, vibrant, exaggerated expressions and absurd scenarios. Can be more playful than standard brand imagery.
- **Market updates:** Chart clips, animated rising arrows, price display graphics.

**Technical specs:** Target 1200x675px for X posts. High-res, clean, uncluttered — one key visual per image.

## CONTENT CATEGORIES & REAL EXAMPLES

### 1. Engagement / Questions
Pose questions to spark replies. High interaction potential.
- Tone: Casual, curious, no judgment. Encourages debate.
- Length: Ultra-short (1-2 sentences)
- No links, no images needed — pure text engagement.
- Example: `You have $1000 to invest in any project right now, what's your pick?`
- Tips: Start with "You have..." or "What's your...". End with "?" to invite responses. Add 👀 for intrigue.

### 2. Advice / Motivational
Short tips or reminders for traders. Empowering and blunt.
- Tone: Professional yet direct. Ultra-short. Use periods for emphasis.
- Length: 1 sentence, under 50 characters ideal
- Example: `Be opinionated. But always DYOR.`
- Tips: Pair with crypto acronyms. Emojis optional (e.g. 📈).

### 3. Feature Announcements
Updates on new tools, integrations, listings.
- Tone: Informative, promotional, excited about user benefits.
- Always highlight the benefit to the user ("easier than ever," "now live")
- Include #BloFinBuild hashtag for feature content
- Link to support.blofin.com for details
- Example: `This #BloFinBuild expands fiat access on BloFin. BTC, ETH, and SOL can now be purchased directly with credit cards, SEPA, or SWIFT making it easier than ever to move your fiat to crypto. Enjoy simplified investing on BloFin 🫡`
- Image: App screenshots in orange/black theme showing UI elements

### 4. Campaigns / Promotions / Giveaways
Events, rewards, invitations with prizes.
- Tone: Enthusiastic, rewarding, urgent
- Specify rewards (e.g. USDT amount, token prizes)
- Include clear entry rules (RT, Like, Follow, Tag friends)
- Example: `Join the BloFin x @partner AMA to learn exclusive trading tips and have a chance to share in 500 USDT rewards 🔥 To enter: RT, Like, @ 3 friends and follow @BloFin_Official. See you there on [date] at [time] UTC.`
- Image: Branded banners with event name, date, rewards, partner logos

### 5. Market Updates
Quick price reports or trend notes with engagement hook.
- Tone: Neutral-factual, then speculative to drive replies
- Format: `$TICKER: Reporting at [price] after a [change] in the last 24 hours 📈 Where's it going from here? 👀`

### 6. Humorous / Memes
Relatable trader life content. The fun side of BloFin.
- Tone: Fun, self-deprecating, light-hearted
- Use meme formats: "No one: [relatable scenario]"
- Relate to trading stress, FOMO, late-night charting, distractions
- Example: `trading charts + this = my weakness` (with image of comfort food)
- Keep humorous without offending

### 7. Recaps / Threads
Summaries of recent events or updates in threaded format.
- Start with hook, list items with 👉 links
- Example: `Keep track on all things BloFin 🫡 Here's a quick recap.`

### 8. Replies / Community Interactions
Short responses to user mentions.
- Length: 1-5 words or emojis only
- Example reply: `🫡` or `gmmm` or `🤝`
- Never promote in replies — just vibe

## POST TEMPLATES

### Twitter Product Feature Template

Used for: Product launches, feature announcements, new tools.

**Layout (16:9, 1280x720):**
- **Background:** Black-to-orange gradient (bottom-right corner glow)
- **Top-left:** BloFin logo + platform badge ("WEB", "APP", "PRO")
- **Left side:** Dark card containing a feature screenshot/product image
- **Right side, top:** TITLE — bold uppercase, orange (#FF8800), 2-3 words max per line
- **Right side, below title:** SUBTITLE — white, regular weight, 1-2 sentences explaining the feature

**Agent output fields:**
- `title`: Short punchy headline in UPPERCASE (e.g. "AUTOMATE YOUR SPOT TRADING")
- `subtitle`: Brief feature explanation
- `image_prompt`: Description of the feature screenshot to display in the left card
- `platform`: Which badge to show — "WEB", "APP", or "PRO"

**Title rules:** ALL CAPS, max 4-5 words, action-oriented (verb first)
**Subtitle rules:** Sentence case, max 2 sentences, explains the what/why
